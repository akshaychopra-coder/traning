GNDEC

Introduction

Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College (GNDEC), established in 1956 in Ludhiana, Punjab, is one of the oldest and most prestigious engineering institutions in northern India. It was founded by the Nankana Sahib Education Trust to promote technical education among rural and underprivileged communities, especially the Sikh minority.

The college is affiliated with I.K. Gujral Punjab Technical University and approved by AICTE.

  • Location: Ludhiana, Punjab, India
  • Campus: 150-acre urban campus
  • Accreditation: NBA
